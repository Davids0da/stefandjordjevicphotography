A handful of stones

/ short fiction / 2017. / 14min /
Ivica is a 11-year-old who spends his days among friends, roaming around unexplored grounds of an industrial town. Bitter and disquieted, he's about to find out that today his parents are separating.
director: Stefan Ivancic
writers: Stefan Djordjevic, Andrijana Stojkovic
dop: Stefan Djordjevic
editors: Dragan von Petrovic, Milena Z. Petrovic
production: Film house Kiselo Dete

Journey

/ short documentary / 2013. / 5min /
A story about an engine driver who travels the same route for eight hours a day. He doesn't see that route in the same way we experience it, because the imagination is the only thing he has in his monotonous surroundings.
director, cinematographer, editor: Stefan Djordjevic

To the border

/ short fiction / 2013. / 24min /
A young couple in love, on vacation, spontaneously find themselves in a fantasy in which they pretend that she is a hitchhiker and he has picked up along the road.
director: Katarina Mutic
written by: Ivan Stancic
dop: Stefan Djordjevic
editor: Rajko Ristanovic

Why do i love skateboarding?

/ short documentary / 2015. / 15min /
A short documentary film about the skateboarding generation shift in Bor. The elder ones who established the local scene are desparetely trying to pass on their values to the young ones, while they have to keep up with their day-to-day grown up life.
director, cinematographer: Stefan Djordjevic
editor: Vanja Kovacevic
production: Vice Serbia
